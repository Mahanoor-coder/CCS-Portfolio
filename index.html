About Continental Cargo Services

Continental Cargo Services (CCS) is a premier international freight forwarding and logistics provider dedicated to bridging the gap between global markets and local businesses. Based in Pakistan, we specialize in delivering seamless, end-to-end supply chain solutions that empower our clients to trade with confidence across borders. With a strategic network spanning from Pakistan to the United States, Panama, and beyond, we don’t just move cargo—we move businesses. Our team of seasoned professionals leverages years of industry expertise to handle everything from complex sea and air freight to meticulous customs brokerage and local distribution.

We specialize in air, sea, and land freight with commitment to timely delivery and secure cargo handling.
